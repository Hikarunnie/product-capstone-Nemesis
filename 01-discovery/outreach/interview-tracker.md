## Outreach Message Templates

### Template A: Friend or classmate

"Hey [Name], I'm working on a project this semester researching how people sell handmade or creative products online. I'm trying to understand your experience using platforms like Instagram. Would you have 20–30 minutes sometime this week for a quick conversation?"

### Template B: Acquaintance or warm introduction

"Hi [Name], [Mutual contact] suggested I reach out. I'm working on a university research project around how people sell handmade or creative products online. I'm looking to speak with people who sell or want to sell through platforms like Instagram. Would you be open to a 20–30 minute chat this week? It would really help our research."

### Template C: Cold outreach (group, forum, community)

"Hi everyone, I'm a student at KIU working on a product development project. I'm researching how people sell handmade or creative products online and would love to speak with anyone who sells or wants to sell through platforms like Instagram. It would take 20–30 minutes. DM me if you'd be up for it. Thanks!"

## Notes and Observations

- Most participants rely on Instagram/Facebook but struggle with **order management and communication**
- Pricing uncertainty is a **major early-stage blocker**
- Many users **never start selling due to psychological barriers**
- Even active sellers experience **workflow chaos and time pressure**
- There is strong demand for a **simple, all-in-one platform**
