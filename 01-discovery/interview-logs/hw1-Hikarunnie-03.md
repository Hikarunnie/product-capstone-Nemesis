## Key Observations

- Observation 1: The participant repeatedly emphasized time limitations, suggesting managing selling process with a full-time job is a great constraint.
- Observation 2: They seemed more frustrated by the selling/communication process than by product creation itself.
- Observation 3: The participant appears motivated to continue selling despite operational difficulties, indicating genuine interest in growing their side business.

---

## Preliminary Insights


- Insight 1: Small handmade art sellers may struggle more with time and operational difficulties than with product creation.
- Insight 2: Manual communication with customers is a recurring pain that may reduce seller efficiency and motivation.
- Insight 3: Existing social selling platforms may feel too complex or unstructured for beginner sellers.
