
**Team Name:** Nemesis
**Problem this script is for:** Artsy individuals in Georgia want to sell handmade or artistic products but lack a structured, trusted platform for selling, pricing, and managing customer interactions. Currently, they rely on Instagram in an unstructured way.
**Date created:** 25 March 2026

---

## Screener Questions

Before scheduling the interview, confirm the match:

1. "Do you currently create handmade or artistic products that you want to sell?" 
2. "Have you tried selling these products online, or are you planning to start soon?"
3. "Do you use Instagram or other social media to showcase your art currently?"

---

## Interview Introduction

"Thanks for taking the time to chat! I’m doing research for a project to understand how creative individuals like you plan to sell handmade products. I’m not selling anything and not pitching a product — I just want to learn about your experience, challenges, and plans. There are no right or wrong answers. Can I take notes while we talk?"

---

## Phase 1: Opening and Context (5 minutes)

**Question 1:** "Can you walk me through your creative process — what you make, how often, and what your goals are for your products?"

**Question 2:** "Have you thought about selling your work? What excites you or worries you about starting?"

**Follow-up prompts ready:**
- "Can you tell me more about that?"
- "How do you imagine this working in the future?"
- "Has it changed since you first started creating?"
---

## Phase 2: Problem Exploration (10 minutes)

**Question 3:** "What makes selling your products feel complicated or intimidating?"

**Question 4:** "If you were to start selling tomorrow, what would be the hardest part for you to handle?"

**Question 5:** "Can you tell me about a time you tried to figure out how to sell online or manage orders? What happened?"

---

## Phase 3: Problem Deep Dive (10 minutes)

**Question 6:** "Walk me through exactly what you would do if you tried to sell one of your pieces online — from showing it off to completing a sale."

**Question 7:** "Why would you do it that way? What made that your approach?"

**Question 8:** "What would happen if you tried to sell and ran into these challenges? How would it affect your plans or motivation?"

---

## Phase 4: Current Solutions (5 minutes)

**Question 9:** "Have you looked at Instagram, Etsy, or other ways to manage selling and pricing? What did you like or not like about them?"

**Question 10:** "If you could change one thing about the process of selling your products, what would it be?"

---

## Phase 5: Close (5 minutes)

**Question 11 (catch-all):** "Is there anything about selling handmade or artistic products that I haven’t asked but you think matters?"
**Referral question:** "Is there anyone else you know who plans to sell their art or handmade items soon? Would you be willing to introduce me?"
**Close:** "This was super helpful — thank you! If something comes up later that’s relevant, is it okay if I send you a quick message?"
